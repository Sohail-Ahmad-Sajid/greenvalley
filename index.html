About Us

Nestled in the heart of lush green hills, GreenValley Hotel & Restaurant has been a destination for travellers seeking peace, comfort, and warm hospitality since 2005.

Our property spans 5 acres of scenic landscape with well-appointed rooms, a multi-cuisine restaurant, and facilities designed to make every stay memorable.

  • 50+ Comfortable Rooms
  • Multi-Cuisine Restaurant
  • Free Wi-Fi & Parking
